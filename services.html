Services

As a Company we oversee all aspects of the installation and servicing, working closely with the customer, architect, installation engineers, electricians and other contractors and subcontractors on site.

We then supervise the commissioning and launch of the system, ironing out any teething problems and providing client personnel with training. Furthermore we are available on demand to assist with evidential material, extra support or help if required.

We are very experienced in diagnosing and rectifying faulty equipment and have the tools to do so.

This may be fibre power and loss meters, fibrescopes, cable length meters, mini monitors, telemetry boards, and cable fault finders.

On networks we have JDSU and Agilent Testers.
